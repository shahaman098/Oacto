# Acceptance Criteria

## MVP Acceptance

- The app loads without runtime errors on desktop and mobile widths.
- The first screen communicates the product outcome immediately.
- A user can inspect one confirmed moving job spec.
- Three vendor quotes are visible and comparable.
- Each quote contains at least one evidence-backed assertion.
- At least one fee or term is flagged as vague, hidden, contradicted, waived, or declined.
- The ledger calculates a comparable all-in total for each vendor.
- The system identifies the best confirmed offer.
- A follow-up negotiation shows a before total, after total, and delta.
- Unsupported leverage is blocked by deterministic logic.
- The full repository verification command passes.
- The live call connects through ElevenLabs with a real conversation ID.
- The agent speaks an AI/transcription disclosure and post-call proof includes provider transcript/audio state.
- A provider or microphone failure is reported in the UI without a simulated-call fallback.

## Judging Acceptance

- The demo can be explained in under 60 seconds.
- The product is not presented as a generic chatbot.
- The AI value is tied to extraction, comparison, and evidence-backed negotiation support.
- The final pitch shows a measurable before/after result.
- Any live API integration has a deterministic fallback for demo reliability.
